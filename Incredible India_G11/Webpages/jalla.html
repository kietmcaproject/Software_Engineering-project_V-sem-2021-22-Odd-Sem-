History of Jalla Hanuman Mandir

Jalla Hanuman Ji Mandir

As per legends, many years ago, there was a desert (Jalla) in this place and there was small ‘Kasbah’ here.
      Once, a Sadhu Baba was passing through this place and because of secluded location he decided to reside here.
At initially he pasted ash from his Dhuna on his hut inside wall and started worshipping as deity. It is said that the main idol of present temple was self manifested at that time when Baba was here.
